# O.W.L.S.-Operational-Agreements

1️⃣ OPERATING AGREEMENT
O.W.L.S., LLC

(Holding Company)

State of Formation: __________
Effective Date: __________

ARTICLE I – FORMATION

O.W.L.S., LLC (“Company”) is organized as a Limited Liability Company under the laws of the State of __________.

ARTICLE II – PURPOSE

The Company is a holding entity formed to:

Own subsidiary entities

Hold intellectual property, trademarks, copyrights, master recordings, publishing rights, brand assets

Provide management and administrative services

Own equity interests in CharlesDub Audio, LLC and Ll Rain Collections, LLC

Engage in lawful business activities

ARTICLE III – MEMBER

Sole Member: Charles __________
Ownership: 100%

The Member has full economic rights.

ARTICLE IV – MANAGEMENT

The Company shall be Manager-Managed.

Initial Manager: Charles __________

Manager has authority to:

Acquire/sell subsidiary equity

Enter financing agreements

Register IP

Approve contracts exceeding $10,000

Hire officers for subsidiaries

Execute licensing agreements

ARTICLE V – SUBSIDIARIES

O.W.L.S., LLC owns:

100% of CharlesDub Audio, LLC

100% of Ll Rain Collections, LLC

Each subsidiary must:

Maintain separate EIN

Maintain separate books

Maintain separate bank accounts

Avoid commingling of funds

Operate under independent contracts

ARTICLE VI – INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All IP created by subsidiaries may be assigned to O.W.L.S., LLC for asset protection.

The Parent may license IP back under written agreements.

ARTICLE VII – LIABILITY

No Member or Manager shall be personally liable for Company debts.

The Company shall indemnify the Member/Manager except in cases of fraud or willful misconduct.

ARTICLE VIII – DISSOLUTION

Dissolution occurs upon:

Written consent of Member

Sale of all subsidiaries

Legal requirement

2️⃣ OPERATING AGREEMENT
CharlesDub Audio, LLC

(Record Label Subsidiary)

Sole Member: O.W.L.S., LLC
Ownership: 100%

PURPOSE

CharlesDub Audio, LLC shall:

Produce and distribute master recordings

Develop artists

Administer publishing

Enter artist agreements

Operate merchandising

Conduct marketing and promotion

MANAGEMENT

Manager: Appointed by O.W.L.S., LLC

Manager authority includes:

Signing artist agreements

Licensing masters

Entering distribution agreements

Running ad campaigns

Hiring producers and contractors

MASTER OWNERSHIP

Unless contractually transferred, all masters produced are owned by CharlesDub Audio, LLC.

The Company may assign masters to O.W.L.S., LLC for holding purposes.

ROYALTY ACCOUNTING

The Company shall:

Maintain separate royalty accounting

Provide quarterly royalty statements

Retain reserves for returns/chargebacks
